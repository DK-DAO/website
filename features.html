Key Features

For Gamer

  • Fair and transparent distribution of in-game items

  • Empowerment and involvement in gaming operations gamers would be able to get involved in game development or give ideas to their favorite games by submitting improvement proposal and get voted/funded by all ChildDAO token holders.

  • Reasonable exchange fee at maximum 50% lower than market rate

  • Authenticated ownership of in-game items: gamers will be provided a cryptographic proof of their in-game item ownership which allows them to be able to freely transfer the items to other people

  • Increased liquidity of in-game items thanks to the transferability of in-game items among gamers and across different games and countries/localities

For Content Creater

  • Potentials to tap in global market to sell their creative assets right away anytime from anywhere

  • Lower exposure to intellectual property rights infringement thanks to the verifiable feature of blockchain and DKDAO. Once an in-game item is verified as an asset of a creator, he or she will have a genesis version of the in-game item issued to them only which makes it impossible for other parties to claim their ow

  • Real-time incentive payout: the creator automatically receives incentives everytime the in-game item changes hands, thanks to a self-deterministic smart contract signed off between the creator and ChildDAO.

For Game Studios

  • Lower fee and higher reinvestment rate for their product game studio can enjoy 50% lower distribution fee than market rate meaning they can reserve bigger funds towards reinvesting and developing their games.

  • Access to a borderless market and exchange to sell/exchange their idea, game and crowdsource to build a new game.

  • Straightforward, verifiable and community-powered scheme of raising fund and distributing proceeds to stakeholders through GrandDAO.

For Investors

  • Access to investment opportunities in a global pool of games.

  • Entitled to dividends allocated from the accrued profit pool of the project.

  • No barriers in terms of investment jurisdictions or cap amount.

  • Participation as a stakeholder in DKDAO with similar voting rights to a stakeholder in a normal company.
